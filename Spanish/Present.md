Present Tense
-----

###Uses

- Something currently being done 
 *Limpia la casa* - *He's tidying the house*
- Regular actions
 *Normalmente, lavo el coche* - *Normally, I wash the car*
- Present descriptions
 *He is strict* - *Es severo*
