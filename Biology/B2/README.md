# 3.4 Unit 2: Biology 2

This covers all of AQA Biology 4401 Unit 2, which can be found on [AQA's website](http://www.aqa.org.uk/subjects/science/gcse/biology-4401/subject-content).  
The Unit is split into 8 Modules:

- **B2.1** Cells and simple cell transport
- **B2.2** Tissues, organs and organ systems
- **B2.3** Photosynthesis
- **B2.4** Organisms and their environment
- **B2.5** Proteins – their functions and uses
- **B2.6** Aerobic and anaerobic respiration
- **B2.7** Cell division and inheritance
- **B2.8** Speciation
